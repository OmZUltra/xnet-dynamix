About Us

Xnet Dynamix Services believes in the power of effective technology to transform your organization, make things work better in the positive direction and take your business to the next level.

Xnet is established as a growing and committed ICT organization in the provision and management of specialist IT infrastructure solutions and services.

Our passion is to find ways to use ICT to make our clients' businesses work better and smarter. We turn their expectations into achievements. We will bring visible value to your business and improve its ability to perform as well. We will position you to respond to today's challenges, with targeted, relevant, innovative services and IT solutions.

We ensure your business runs on a reliable and effective networking platform to help you optimize performance and availability of the collaboration, unified communication tools and your applications that run on it. We can also assist to put in place a next generation data center, support your move to cloud computing, and ensure that all elements of your infrastructure are secured.

Our team is made of highly skilled and experienced professionals who have distinguished themselves in their respective areas of specializations, such as Oracle Financials, Oracle Human Resources, Oracle Database management and Oracle E- Business Suite Integration with other Applications.

Our Oracle Financial Consultants are all Oracle Certified Experts with successful implementation of Oracle E-Business Suite for both large and medium corporations including the Nigerian Ports Authority etc.

These capabilities are supported by a full lifecycle of IT services encompassing consulting, professional, support, managed, training, procurement and supply chain services. The result is a stable yet agile technology estate - one that sets you up to embrace business changes with confidence.
